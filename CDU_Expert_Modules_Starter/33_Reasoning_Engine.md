# Reasoning Engine

## Core Algorithm

Observe

↓

Collect Evidence

↓

Generate Hypotheses

↓

Rank Confidence

↓

Choose Experiment

↓

Predict Result

↓

Run HYSYS

↓

Compare Prediction vs Reality

↓

Update Confidence

↓

Repeat

## Confidence Score

Each hypothesis stores:

- Supporting evidence
- Contradicting evidence
- Historical success
- Engineering priority
- Risk

Confidence shall be recalculated after every iteration.
